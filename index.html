Wild Animals

- Blog about nature -



Let's talk about wild animals around the world:

Giraffe

Area: Africa

Weight: 900-1200kg

Height: 6m


Elephant

Area: Africa, Asia

Weight: 4000-7000kg

Height: 3m


Lion

Area: Africa

Weight: 150-250kg

Height: 1.2m

Fun fact: Lions are the only cats that live in groups called prides.


Tiger

Area: Asia

Weight: 90-310kg

Height: 70-120cm

Fun fact: Tigers are excellent swimmers and often cool off in pools or streams.


Wolf

Area: North America, Europe, Asia

Weight: 30-80kg

Height: 60-90cm

Fun fact: Wolves can run at speeds of up to 60 km/h
